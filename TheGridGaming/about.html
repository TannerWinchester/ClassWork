About Us

Here at Grid Gaming we are a group of enthusiasts that want to see dreams come true. We created the most elaborate gaming arcade you can find, When you join our club you will get access to all of it!

Hyper-Reality

Hyper-Reality is a term used for a virtual reality experience that tricks your senses into beleiving you truly left this world and entered the realm of your game. We use cutting edge tech and set up a course where you adventure through paired with the VR headset and the game we create for you.

Gaming Center

We realise that the hyper-reality experience can only be suited for small groups at a time, which is why we also accomodate the traditional type of gaming that we all have come to love. We have an entire section set up with Playstations, Xbox, and also custom made PC's for our club members to hang out and play competitive games. Or, if you rather just relax and play single player games to kill time we have that too.

Arcade Area

We are true gamers here and dont expect everyone to fall in the same catagory as consoles or PC's so we also offer exclusive arcade games to our members! If you would rather play some fun arcade games with your friends this is the place to be.
